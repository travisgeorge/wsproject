Project Analysis

Eric's Pet Services

Site Intent

Overview

Eric's Pet Services provides care to your pets and animals while you are away and unable. These services include daily dog walking, pet visits, pet/house sitting, exotic animal care, and care for special needs pets. The purpose of the website is to detail what services Eric's Pet Services, Inc. has to offer as well as help the user get in contact with the business, via phone call or text message, to schedule an appointment.

Client Goals

Eric's Pet Services is looking to create a warmer, modern and up to date, more inviting, and more professional online appearance through this site redesign. While at the same time demonstrating the businesses reliability, credibility, and price compared to competitors.
